Who Are We?

Albanian Guitars is a distinguished company dedicated to the art of crafting high-quality musical instruments. Specializing in acoustic and electric guitars, violins, and custom-made guitars, we take pride in delivering instruments that resonate with the soul of the musician.

Our instruments are crafted from a variety of exquisite Albanian woods, each carefully selected for its acoustic properties and visual appeal. Some of the woods we use include Chestnut, Walnut, Maple and Juniper.
